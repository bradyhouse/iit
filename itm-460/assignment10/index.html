Joy of Stop Animation

There is no joy. The first minute of Episode #1 took literally a hundred hours of my life. The second and third minutes are simply a still shot with injected video. After admitting defeat and handing in the assignment, I realized that video is not my medium--ie I will never try this again. Lesson learned? Ummm, if you really want to get into stop animation, you will need more than just a Mac and iMovie.
