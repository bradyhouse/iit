First Attempt - Crash and Burn

This was my first attempt at meshing video with audio using iMovie. As you can see its a mess. This was before I realized you could stretch a single audio clip across multiple video clips. Once I realized this, I had to start over. Interestingly though, this failed attempt demonstrates what happens when you import clips over and over into iMovie. With each import, the clips become grayer (or more faded).
